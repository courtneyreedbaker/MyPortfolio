Integra Training

Fitness - Usability - Heuristic Analysis

Problem

To improve the usability of their website to encourage people to sign up for a consultation with their personal training company.

Process

I was asked to conduct a usability evaluation on their website. I conducted user surveys and a heuristic analysis of their website using the 10 measures from the Nielsen-Norman group to generate some concrete recommendations to improve their website.

Results

Integra Training implemented an up front CTA for scheduling a consultation, a clearer explanation of fitness approach, eliminated confusing phrasing, eliminated irrelevant imagery, and improved the tone of testimonials.



Consultation Objectives

  1. Conduct user surveys

  2. Conduct a heuristic analysis

  3. Provide concrete recommendations for their website

Research Findings

The aesthetic in general was favorable, but navigating the website was not intuitive. Getting people to sign up for a consultation was the main stumbling block as 37.5% of users felt it wasn't in the place they imagined and 25% couldn't find it.

Heuristic Analysis

The heuristic analysis measures are 10 measures taken from the Nielsen-Normal Group. The usability measures are listed below with the corresponding numbers on the heuristic analysis that I conducted for Integra Training.

  1. Visibility of system status

  2. Match between system and the real world

  3. User control and freedom

  4. Consistency and standards

  5. Error prevention

  6. Recognition rather than recall

  7. Flexibility and efficiency of use

  8. Aesthetics and minimalist design

  9. Help users to recognize, diagnose and recover from errors

  10. Help and documentation

Recommendations

  1. Simplify the structure of the website.

  2. Include some pictures of the facility and the staff at work.

  3. Make sure your mission statement is clear and simple.

  4. Move the features and the quotes down a bit.

Implementation

  1. Up front CTA for scheduling a consultation

  2. Clearer explanation of fitness approach

  3. Eliminating confusing phrasing

  4. Eliminating irrelevant imagery

  5. Improving the tone of testimonials
