Courtney Baker | About Me

Background in Evaluation

Prior to working UX, I worked as an educational evaluator. This has given me insight in how research is conducted and a strong focus on the importance of user research in my design practice.

UX Design Consultant

During my time as a consultant, I've worked with a wide variety of clients from ideating start ups to large enterprise companies like IBM. My clients consistently comment on their satisfaction with my services and on my ability to deliver thorough solutions.

Current Goals and Efforts

I am actively looking to join a UX design team full-time. In the mean time, I am continuing to broaden my toolkit by working as a user researcher with a start up called u-Tapp and taking a Visual Design course at the UC Berkeley Extension in San Francisco this Summer.
