Storefront - Metrics - Design Challenge

Problem

Create a design for a pet shop website that includes the front end interaction and a backend metrics tracking.

Process

I was given the design prompt by an interviewing company and was required to finish designs within the week. I did competitive analysis to make sure my feature sets were complete, brainstorming, and wireframes for the front end and back end screens.

Results

The interviewers felt the design solutions were extremely thorough and appreciated the amount of thought that went into each page.



Design Challenge Criteria

  1. Create a website that showcases all the listed animals.

  2. Make sure the customers can see how many of each animal are available.

  3. Admins should be able to keep track of the flow of their inventory (animals).

  4. Customer facing should include a home page and an individual animal page at minimum.

  5. Admin facing should include a dashboard, animals separated by type, and metrics to track the adoption rates.

Competitive Analysis

I compared Amazon, PetSmart, Petco and Petfinder to gather features that I wanted to include in my application design. I looked at Amazon to help with information architecture choices and the other three to look for ways to format the information as well as what information people look for when shopping for pet related products.

Planning

When I plan for a design, my favorite starting point is to write out all the elements of the project and start to create a hierarchy. I sketch basic ideas out and write out a variety of ideas that I may want to incorporate into the design.

Branding

  • Primary Colors: Blue, orange, yellow

  • Logo: Multi-animal logo

  • Brand Qualities: Fun, welcoming, family-friendly, compassionate, adoption oriented

  • Font: Nunito Light (rounded, soft)

Low-Fidelity Wireframes

Storefront Designs

  • Filters by quality and openly displayed number of pets by type

  • Strong mission statement to support brand qualities

  • Visual iconography for easy scanning

Backend Designs

  • Upfront notifications and metrics tracking on the dashboard

  • Smart suggestions based on search history and inquiries

  • Compact information display for easy scanning

  • Continuous aesthetic from front to back end for a more enjoyable experience

Results

Though the team decided to go with another candidate, they were positive about the design I proposed mentioning specifically my attention to detail.
